    Njego Mausoleum in Loven National Park, Montenegro
    joci03/iStock/Getty Images
    Atop Mount Loven is a mausoleum housing the remains of Petar II Petrovi-Njego, a 19th-century philosopher and poet whose epic poems had a lasting influence on the literature of both Montenegro and Serbia. Njego was granted the title of prince-bishop and ruled Montenegro for a time, hoping to unite Serbian people across the Balkans. Hes as venerated for his political and religious leadership as he is for his literature. Learn more
